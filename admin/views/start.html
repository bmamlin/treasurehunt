Since we're having a treasure hunt, you need to be ready to be found! There are three things you need to learn from this page:

  1. Make sure your QR scanner is working.
  2. Know what to expect when you scan someone's QR code and how to handle problems.
  3. Know the no proxies rule

Use the settings of your QR app to (1) turn of sound and (2) automatically open in your default browser. These settings, combined with temporarily placing the app where you can get to it quickly, will make it easy for you to scan players' QR codes.

How the game works

Players register at the Regensrief booth and pick up a sheet with all of our pictures and their own, unique QR code on it. Then they try to find each of us and have us scan their QR code. The top 25% of the most successful players will be put in a raffle to win an iPad.

You should expect people to approach you during the meeting asking you to scan their QR code, so please keep your phone with you and charged at all times during AMIA.

When you scan a code, you should see their name on the screen, which gives you the chance to introduce yourself and meet them. If their name hasn't been entered yet, take a moment to enter their name and organization.

No proxies rule

Please only scan the QR code for the person in front of you. If someone comes up to you with multiple QR codes or someone else's QR code(s), politely tell them that you are only allowed to scan their QR code.

The point of this game is to get players to meet you. If you aren't meeting the person, then they're not playing the game.

Have fun!

If you have any problems, report them to Burke or Sandy.
