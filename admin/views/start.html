Get started in three easy steps:

  1. Make sure your QR scanner is working.
  2. Know what to expect when someone asks you to scan their QR code.

1. Make sure your QR scanner is working

Load http://regen.st/thtest on a computer or someone else's phone and scroll down to the test player's QR code

  • We recommend installing QuickMark. See "Configuring QuickMark" below.
  • iPhone users (as of iOS 11) can use the built-in camera; however, you can still save a tap by using QuickMark.

Scanning the QR code should take you to the test player's game page where you should see a green bar at the top:

Awarded

If you do not see the green bar, see troubleshooting below.

2. How the game works

Players register at our booth and get a sheet with all of our pictures and their unique QR code on it. They "hunt" for each of us to have us scan their QR code. The top 25% of the most successful players are put in a raffle to win a prize.

You should expect people to approach you during the meeting asking you to scan their QR code, so please keep your phone with you and charged at all times during AMIA.

When you scan a code, look for the green bar and their name on the screen, then introduce yourself and meet them.

3. The "no proxies" rule

Please only scan the QR code for the person in front of you. If someone comes up to you with multiple QR codes or someone else's QR code(s), politely tell them that you are only allowed to scan their QR code.

The point of this game is to get players to meet you. If you aren't meeting the person, then they're not playing the game.

Have fun!

If you have any problems, report them to Burke or Sandy.

Configuring QuickMark

A properly configured QR reader makes this game much simpler. Ideally, when someone shows you a QR code, all you have to do is point your phone at it without any additional taps. Install QuickMark and go into the settings to make these changes (based on iPhone, I'm assuming Android would offer the same):

  1. Auto Open > Browse URL: On
  2. Browser: Safari/Chrome (not In App)
  3. Sound: Off

Troubleshooting

Trouble scanning a QR code

Use the built-in camera for iOS 11+ or install QuickMark on your phone. If you are using a QR Scanner App, make sure it is configured to open URLs in the native browser (not an embedded) browser.

Not seeing the green bar

If scanning the QR code takes you to the test player's page, but you do not see the green bar at the top, make sure you are logged in. There is a login option under the QR code.

If logging in doesn't seem to work, make sure you are not using your browser in private mode. Authentication will not work in private mode.

Any other problems

Ask Burke or Sandy for help.
